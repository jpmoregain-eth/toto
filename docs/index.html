Privacy Policy

App name: TOTO SG
Developer: The JPMoreGain Project
Last updated: 22 March 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how TOTO SG ("the app") handles information when you use our application.

Information We Collect

TOTO SG does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information from its users. The app is a read-only lottery results viewer and number generator. We do not require you to create an account or provide any personal details to use the app.

Third-Party Services

TOTO SG uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google AdMob may collect certain information to serve relevant ads, including device identifiers and usage data. This data collection is governed by Google's Privacy Policy, which can be found at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

Data Sources

TOTO SG displays lottery results data sourced from Singapore Pools. All data displayed in the app is publicly available information. The app does not scrape or misuse this data in any way that violates Singapore Pools' terms of service.

Children's Privacy

TOTO SG is not intended for use by children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. As this app relates to lottery and gambling information, it is intended for adults only.

Internet Access

The app requires internet access solely to retrieve lottery results data from our database and to display advertisements. No personal data is transmitted during this process.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
